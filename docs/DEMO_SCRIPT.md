# Katiba OS — 4-minute live demo

## Opening — 20 seconds

“Amina supplied goods, was partly paid, and is still owed KES 86,000. Her evidence is scattered across an invoice, M-Pesa, WhatsApp, and a delivery note. Katiba OS does not give her a chatbot answer. It turns that evidence into a reviewable legal workflow.”

## 1. Citizen journey — 65 seconds

1. On the landing page, select **Explore citizen demo**.
2. Point out the calm language, current case, readiness, and single next action.
3. Open case **KO-2026-0042**.
4. Show the evidence-linked chronology. Open one event and connect it to its source item.
5. Show **AI findings**: strengths, missing service address, and the explicit human-review boundary.
6. Show the allow-listed Kenya Law and Judiciary sources. Say: “The model organizes facts; the product controls citations.”

## 2. Tangible output — 35 seconds

1. Select **Download preparation pack**.
2. Explain the three pages: case summary, evidence index/chronology, and human checklist.
3. Say: “This is preparation, not automated filing. A lawyer or paralegal must verify it.”

## 3. Lawyer workflow — 50 seconds

1. Leave the demo and select **Open lawyer workspace**.
2. Show the shared review queue, readiness signal, missing facts, and source-backed analysis.
3. Change the case status only after explaining the professional review gate.
4. Say: “Citizens get clarity; lawyers get structured facts and provenance instead of an unfiltered AI transcript.”

## 4. Unified platform — 40 seconds

1. Open **Platform**.
2. Introduce the four engines: Justice, Contract, Compliance, Evidence.
3. Open **Contracts** and show health score, high-risk clauses, and obligations.
4. Say: “One identity, one audit layer, one evidence standard—multiple legal workflows.”

## 5. Technical credibility — 45 seconds

Open **Trust center** and call out:

- signed, expiring sessions and role enforcement;
- schema validation and normalized legal data;
- evidence checksums and immutable-style audit events;
- structured AI output with a deterministic fallback;
- PWA and accessibility support;
- production migration path to PostgreSQL, encrypted object storage, queues, MFA, and tenant isolation.

## Close — 25 seconds

“Katiba OS turns legal complexity into accountable action. It helps a citizen understand the next step, gives legal professionals a faster review surface, and creates reusable infrastructure for contracts and compliance. The ask is a pilot with a legal-aid clinic and an SME legal team so we can measure preparation time, completion, and reviewer effort.”

## Recovery lines

- If live AI is unavailable: “The safe deterministic mode is intentional; legal workflows must degrade safely.”
- If the API is restarted: refresh and re-enter the one-click role demo.
- If time is short: citizen case → evidence map → PDF → lawyer review → close.
